Welcome to 4F: Fantastical, fast, and furious fantasy leagues


About

This website was created by Silvia Oliveros as part of her Insight Data Engineering project.

Motivation

4F Sports combines two of my favorite things: data and sports. While focusing on the Data Engineering aspect, I thought it would be interesting to learn about the implications of trying to develop a pipeline that updates with real-time events and serves a user base of ~ 5 million people.

I engineered data that simulates 5 million users who are part of a fantasy league and had drafted 4 real football players. I replay NFL data from the 2014 season to simulate real-time events and update my user's fantasy points in real-time. I also run some batch queries to find my top users every day.

My technology stack includes: Kafka, HDFS, Spark, Spark Streaming, and Cassandra.
